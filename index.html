Frequently asked questions

What exactly happens on the free audit call?
It's a 30-minute conversation. We ask about your business, where you spend the most repetitive time, how leads come in, and what tools you already use. At the end you'll have a clear picture of what could be automated and whether any of our services are a fit. No hard sell — if it's not a match, we'll say so.
How long does setup take?
Typically 5–10 business days from the time we have access to your accounts and all the info we need. We'll review the automation with you before anything goes live.
What if I want to cancel?
Monthly retainers require 30 days notice. There's no long-term contract. If you cancel, we hand back everything we built — your automations, credentials, and documentation — cleanly.
Do I need any technical knowledge?
No. You give us access to the tools you already use (email, Instagram, WhatsApp, your CRM, etc.) and we handle the rest. We document everything in plain language so you always know what's running.
Will customers know they're talking to an AI?
That's your call. We can configure the AI to introduce itself or to reply in a way that matches your brand voice. Many clients prefer a clear "assistant" label; others integrate it seamlessly. We discuss this together during setup and follow your preference — and local laws around disclosure.
What's GEO and why does it matter?
GEO stands for Generative Engine Optimization — how your brand appears in AI-generated answers (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity). More people are skipping Google and asking AI directly. Our GEO Snapshot tells you what AI says about your business today and what to change to show up better over time.
Can I combine services?
Yes. Many clients start with the AI Front Desk and add GEO Snapshot once it's running. We'll recommend what makes sense for your situation on the audit call.
